When To Consult A Specialist

Cardiology: chest discomfort, breathlessness, high-risk blood pressure cases.

Neurology: persistent headaches, stroke symptoms, seizures, memory concerns.

Orthopedics: joint pain, injury, mobility limitations, trauma care follow-up.

Pediatrics and Neonatology: child growth, infections, vaccination, NICU-linked follow-up.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right doctor at HUI Hospital?

Choose by department and specialization, then use the online booking flow for that specialist.

Can I book with a specific specialist online?

Yes. Clicking Book on a doctor profile auto-selects the related department in the booking form.

Are follow-up visits supported?

Yes, follow-up consultations can be scheduled using the same appointment process.
